Employee Onboarding

Your first 90 days.

A guided journey from Day 1 to Month 3 — click through each stage.

Course 01

Complete Claude 101

Your first step into working with Claude, Anthropic's AI assistant. Learn what Claude is, how to start a conversation, and how to write clear, effective prompts. Covers the essentials of everyday use — asking questions, summarising, drafting and iterating — so you can put AI to work from day one.

Go to course ↗
Course 02

Introduction to Claude & Cowork

Go beyond the basics into collaborative, agentic workflows. Understand how Cowork lets Claude take on multi-step tasks, work alongside your team and plug into the tools you already use — turning Claude from a chatbot into a genuine working partner across your day-to-day projects.

Go to course ↗
Course 03

AI Capabilities & Limitations

Build sound judgement for using AI responsibly. Learn what today's models do well, where they fall short, and how to spot & guard against issues like hallucinations and bias. Covers verifying outputs, protecting confidential data, and knowing when a human must stay in the loop.

Go to course ↗
